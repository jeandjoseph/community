#Basic Prompts

1. “Summarize + Action Plan” Prompt
Prompt: “Summarize the last 10 unread emails from my project folder, highlight any deadlines or decisions I need to make, and draft a prioritized action list I can copy into my planner.”

Why it impresses: Copilot pulls context across multiple emails, extracts commitments, and turns chaos into a clean, actionable plan.

2. “Rewrite for Tone + Audience” Prompt
Prompt: “Rewrite this email to sound confident, concise, and executive‑ready. Keep the key message, but make it more persuasive and remove unnecessary details.”
Why it impresses: Shows how Copilot adapts tone, clarity, and professionalism instantly — a huge win for business users.

3. “Draft From Scattered Notes” Prompt
Prompt: “Using the notes below, draft a polished follow‑up email to the client summarizing what we agreed on, next steps, and what we need from them before Friday.” (Paste messy notes underneath during the demo.)

Why it impresses: Copilot transforms rough, unstructured text into a clean, client‑ready message — a real “wow” moment.

4. “Draft to send meeting invite” Prompt
Use the context from my current email thread to draft a professional message proposing a meeting with the customer. Check my calendar for availability next week and provide three different dates, each with a specific time that fits my schedule. Provide a polished email draft asking the customer to confirm their preferred option by replying to this thread.


#Advance Prompts
1. Multi‑Threaded Insight Extraction Across a Project
Prompt: “Analyze all emails from the last 30 days related to the ‘Data Modernization Initiative.’ Identify the major themes, risks, blockers, and any conflicting information between stakeholders. Then draft a concise briefing I can send to leadership with recommended next steps.”

Why it’s advanced: Copilot performs cross‑thread analysis, resolves contradictions, and produces an executive‑ready briefing — something that normally takes hours.

2. Generate a Decision‑Ready Comparison From Email Attachments
Prompt: “Review all attachments in the last 5 emails from the vendor shortlist. Create a comparison summary highlighting pricing, scope, timelines, assumptions, and red flags. Then draft a recommendation email I can send to procurement.”

Why it’s advanced: Copilot reads attachments, extracts structured data, and synthesizes it into a decision framework plus a ready‑to‑send email.

3. Turn a Chaotic Inbox Into a Strategic Weekly Plan
Prompt: “Scan my inbox for any tasks, commitments, or follow‑ups I owe this week. Group them by project, estimate urgency, and create a weekly execution plan with priorities, dependencies, and suggested deadlines. Draft it in a format I can paste into Planner or OneNote.”

Why it’s advanced: Copilot becomes a personal operations assistant — extracting obligations, organizing them, and producing a structured plan.
